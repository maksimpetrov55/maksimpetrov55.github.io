Penalty on late and overdue instalments
+
?
You will be charged instalment interest if all the following apply:

1) You are required to pay by instalments in 2021
2) You receive an instalment reminder in 2021 that shows an amount to pay
3) You did not make any of your instalment payments, paid late, or paid less than what you had to pay

CRA charge instalment interest on all late or insufficient instalment payments. Instalment interest is compounded daily at the prescribed interest rate, which can change every 3 months.
1) Failing to pay the required instalment on time results in instalment interest of 5% charged on the difference between required instalment and what was actually paid and compounded daily.

2) Instalment penalty is applied only when instalment interest is more than $1,000. Instalment penalty is the greater of:
    a) $1,000
    b) 25% x instalment interest if you had not made any instalment payments for 2021.

Example: CRA calculated instalment interest for the year is $2,000 and $5,000 if no instalments made.
$2,000 - $1,250 (greater of: $1,000 or $5,000 x 25%) / 2
= $375 as instalment penalty in addition to instalment interest

Note - In Quebec additional interest of 10% per annum, compounded daily is applicable where the amount of an instalment payment is less than 75% of the amount the taxpayer is required to pay.
