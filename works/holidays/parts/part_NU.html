Public Holiday Eligibility Criteria
+
To qualify for pay for a Public Holiday, individuals must meet all of the following:
1) The employee has not worked for the same employer for a total of thirty days during the preceding twelve months prior to the holiday;
2) The employee did not report to work on that day after having been called to work on that day;
3) Without the consent of his or her employer, the employee has not reported for work on either his or her last regular working day preceding or following the general holiday;
4) The employee is on pregnancy or parental leave

Important Notes
+
1) If the employee qualifies for the holiday and the holiday falls on the employee’s regular day off, the employer must give the employee a different day off with pay.
2) If employee worked on the holiday, but agreed to take a day in lieu instead of being paid out, then Holiday Pay amount is deferred until the day in lieu is taken.
