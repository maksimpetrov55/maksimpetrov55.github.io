Public Holiday Eligibility Criteria
+
To qualify for pay for a Public Holiday, individuals must meet all of the following:
1) Be employed for/with the same employer for 30 calendar days prior to the holiday 
2) Have earned pay on at least 15 of the 30 calendar days before the holiday
3) Have worked his/her last scheduled shift before the holiday and his/her first scheduled shift after the holiday

Important Notes
+
1) If the employee qualifies for the holiday and the holiday falls on the employee’s regular day off, the employer must give the employee a different day off with pay.
2) If employee worked on the holiday, but agreed to take a day in lieu instead of being paid out, then Holiday Pay amount is deferred until the day in lieu is taken.
