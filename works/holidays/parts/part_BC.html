Important Notes
+
1) Employees don't need to work the day before and the day after to qualify for statutory holiday pay
2) Easter Sunday, Easter Monday and Boxing Day are not statutory holidays in B.C.
3) If an employee does not qualify for statutory holiday pay, they are paid regular pay for working on a statutory holiday.
4) Employees should be paid statutory holiday pay (an average day's pay) for a regular or scheduled day off that falls on a statutory holiday.
