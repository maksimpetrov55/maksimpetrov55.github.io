Public Holiday Eligibility Criteria
+
To qualify for pay for a Public Holiday, individuals must meet all of the following:
1) holiday occurs within 30 days following the beginning of the employee's employment;
2) where the employee fails, without just cause or the consent of the employer, to comply with the contract of service on the regular work day immediately preceding and immediately after, the public holiday, or either of those work days.

Important Notes
+
1) If a holiday falls on a non-working day or during an employee’s vacation, the employee who qualifies must receive another working day off with pay in lieu of the holiday, or if the employee agrees, a regular day’s pay for that day 2) If employee worked on the holiday, but agreed to take a day in lieu instead of being paid out, then Holiday Pay amount is deferred until the day in lieu is taken.
