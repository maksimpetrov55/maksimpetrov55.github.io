Public Holiday Eligibility Criteria
+
1) An employee is entitled to general holiday pay if they have worked for the same employer for at least 30 workdays in the 12 months prior to the holiday
AND 1 of the following apply:
1) holiday falls on a regular day of work, or
2) worked on a holiday that is not a regular day of work

An employee is not entitled to holiday pay when:
1) they do not work on a holiday but are required or scheduled to do so
2) are absent from employment without consent of the employer on the employee’s last regular working day preceding, or first regular working day following of the holiday
