Public Holiday Eligibility Criteria
+
All employees receive general holiday pay (regardless of length of time employee work for an employer) unless:
1) They are scheduled to work on a general holiday, but are absent without the employer's permission.
OR
2) They are absent without the employer's permission from their last scheduled workday before the holiday, or their first scheduled workday after the holiday.
OR
3) If employees end the employment before a general holiday there is no entitlement to general holiday pay for that holiday.
