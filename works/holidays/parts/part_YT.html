Public Holiday Eligibility Criteria
+
To qualify for pay for a Public Holiday, individuals must meet all of the following:
1) Employed 30 calendar days before the holiday.
2) Work your last scheduled shift before the holiday and your first scheduled shift after the holiday, unless your absence is permitted by the ESA (like sick leave or a day off that you requested and approved)
3) You are required to work on the holiday if called to work, but would be entitled to additional pay depending on whether you work regular hours and how you're paid.

Employee Agree to Take a Day in Lieu?
?
An employee who works on a holiday and who is qualified to be paid holiday pay is entitled to receive the following:
A regular day's pay; plus one and one-half times the employee's regular rate of wages for the number of hours worked on that holiday;
OR
A regular rate of wages for the number of hours worked on that day; plus, another day off with the employee's regular day of pay on a date agreed upon by the employer and employee before the employee's next paid vacation.
