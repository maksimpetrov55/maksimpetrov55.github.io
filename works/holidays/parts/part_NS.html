Public Holiday Eligibility Criteria
+
To qualify for pay for a Public Holiday, individuals must meet all of the following: 1) be entitled to receive pay for at least 15 of the 30 calendar days before the holiday 2) have worked on their last scheduled shift or day before the holiday and on the first scheduled shift or day after the holiday
