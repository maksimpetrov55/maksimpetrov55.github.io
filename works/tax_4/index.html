Penalty on late and overdue instalments
+
1) Failure to file GST/HST return with amount owing to CRA will result in penalty
Example: GST/HST Owing is $1,500 and was overdue for 5 months. Penalty would be $33.75 = $1,500 x 1% + ($1,500 x 1% x 25% x 5 months)"

2) Demand to file is sent by CRA if your GST/HST return is overdue and you fail to file by CRA specified date, a flat $250 penalty applies.

3) Failure to file electronically a flat fee of $100 for initial return and $250 for any subsequent return

4) Failing to pay the required instalment on time results in instalment interest of 5% charged on the difference between required instalment and what was actually paid and compounded daily.
Important Notes
+
1) If your business has branches or divisions that file separate returns, the threshold limit of $3,000 applies to the total net tax for the whole business, including all branches and divisions.
